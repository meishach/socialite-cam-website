How It Works

Five steps. One unforgettable brand moment.

No app to download, no line to stand in — just a roaming team turning your event into branded content your guests can't wait to share.

1

Roam & Shoot

We come to you! Attendants roam your event space taking group photos of your guests — no booth, no setup or breakdown, no footprint.

2

Brand the Border

Every photo is wrapped in a custom border our expert design team builds around your logo, event theme, and message.

3

Instant Delivery

Guests enter their own number and get their branded photo, plus a personalized message, texted to their phone in seconds.

4

Exponential Reach

Guests share their photos across their favorite social media accounts, multiplying your brand's exposure organically.

5

Boost it Baby!

Give guests a reason to post: a discount code, a sweepstakes entry, or a shot at winning merch or tickets, and turn casual posting into a tsunami of sharing.

Your own live event dashboard

  • Self-service border approval — no waiting on email back-and-forth, approve your design directly, with revision history if you want changes.
  • Message control — you write what guests actually receive, not a locked template.
  • Platform-by-platform share tracking — not just "shares," but which platform, in real time.
  • Live photo feed — watch guest photos land in your dashboard the moment they're taken, then download any shot to display live on screen at your event.
  • Live sweepstakes — run your drawing right in the middle of the event, not after it's over.

Consent and trust — built into every text.

Every photo delivery runs on a TCPA-compliant, Twilio-verified toll-free messaging program. Guests choose to enter their own number, so trust is part of the experience from the first text.
